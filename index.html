Hundreds of Bands

From the Coachella Main Stage to the Safari Dance, the Coachella Music Festival brings hundreds of differents bands from all genres of music to the California desert for 3 nights of unforgettable music. No matter your favorite type of tunes, Coachella has something for everyone!

Music in the Desert

Coachella Music Festival is located in Indio, Ca., just 20 minutes east of Palm Springs, at the Empire Polo Fields. It spans 2 weekends every April and people from all over the globe travel to the desert for this amazing experience of music and art. Whether you enjoy on site camping, or staying in a hotel, Coachella offers several options for how to enjoy your music filled weekend.

Five Stages of Entertainment

  1. Main Stage - Headliner's Stage
  2. Outdoor Stage
  3. Gobi Tent
  4. Mojave Tent
  5. Safari Tent - "The Dance Tent"
